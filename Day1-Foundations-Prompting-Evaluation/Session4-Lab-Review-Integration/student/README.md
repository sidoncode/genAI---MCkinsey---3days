# Session 4 — Student Guide

## Day 1 Lab Review & Integration

### What to Expect

This is a **facilitated discussion session** — no notebook or coding is required. Your instructor will lead a review and integration exercise covering all Day 1 concepts.

### What to Bring

- Your completed (or in-progress) notebooks from Sessions 1–3
- Notes on any questions or challenges you encountered
- Ideas for how you would apply today's techniques to real McKinsey consulting work

### Discussion Topics

1. Designing a consulting AI system using Day 1 techniques
2. Evaluating AI-generated consulting deliverables for partner quality
3. Connecting embeddings, prompting, and evaluation into a cohesive system
4. Previewing Day 2 frameworks (LangChain, LangGraph, multi-agent systems)

### Reflection Questions

- Which prompting technique was most useful for consulting analysis?

- What evaluation criteria matter most for different types of consulting work?
